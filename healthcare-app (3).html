⚙️ EmailJS Setup Guide
EmailJS lets you send emails directly from the browser — no backend server needed. It's free for up to 200 emails/month.
Step 1 — Create free account
Go to emailjs.com and sign up for free.
Open EmailJS ↗
Step 2 — Add Email Service
Dashboard → Email Services → Add New Service → choose Gmail/Outlook → connect your email account → copy the Service ID.
Step 3 — Create Email Template
Dashboard → Email Templates → Create New. Use this template body:
Subject: Your MedCare OTP Code Hello, Your OTP for MedCare login is: {{otp_code}} This code expires in 60 seconds. Do not share it with anyone. – MedCare Team
Set To Email field as: {{to_email}} — then copy the Template ID.
Step 4 — Get Public Key
Dashboard → Account → General → copy your Public Key.
Step 5 — Paste Keys Below
✅ EmailJS configured! Real OTPs will now be sent to user emails.
